# spctl orders cancel

## Usage:

```shell
$ spctl orders cancel <id> [--config <config>]
```

cancel order

## Description:

Cancel order with specified id

## Arguments:

|**Name**|**Description**|
| :- | :- |
|`id`|ID of the order|

## Options:

|**Name, shorthand**|**Default**|**Description**|
| :- | :- | :- |
|`--config`|`./config.json`|Path to a config file|
